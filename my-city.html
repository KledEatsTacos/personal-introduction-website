City

Jeddah, known as the 'Bride of the Red Sea', is a vibrant city on Saudi Arabia's coast. It offers a unique blend of historic districts and modern architecture. As the gateway for pilgrimages to Mecca and Medina, it's a city of cultural significance and diversity.

About City

Jeddah is a port city located along the Red Sea coast. As of 2022, it has a population of about 3,751,722 people, making it the second largest city in Saudi Arabia (After the capital Riyadh). Jeddah is also a gateway to the two most holy cities in Islam, Mecca and Medina. The city is known for its historic districts, modern architecture, and vibrant culture. It is a popular destination for tourists and pilgrims alike.
